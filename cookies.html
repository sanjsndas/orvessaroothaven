Cookies Policy
tending digital footprints

Like the hidden mycelium beneath our gardens, cookies help us understand how you interact with our haven. This policy explains what we collect, why we use it, and how you maintain control over your digital soil.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and improve your experience. In the spirit of monochrome gardening — cookies function like subtle markers in the soil: they help us see what thrives and where to nurture a smoother journey.

2. How we use cookies at Orvessa Root Haven

We use cookies to ensure our website functions properly, to analyze anonymous usage patterns, and to honor your browsing preferences. Because we value clarity and contrast, we never sell your data or use intrusive tracking for advertising. The types of cookies we deploy include:

  • Essential Strictly necessary cookies — these enable core functionality such as page navigation, form submissions, and secure areas. Without them, the site would not perform correctly.
  • Analytical Performance & analytics cookies — we gather aggregated data about how visitors use our pages (e.g., most visited articles, garden workshop interest). This helps us refine our content and atelier offerings.
  • Preference Functional cookies — remember selections like language or if you’ve dismissed our cookie banner, making your visit more seamless.

We do not use marketing or third-party targeting cookies that track your activity across unrelated websites. Your digital privacy aligns with our philosophy: slow, intentional, and respectful.

🌿 Rooted transparency: Any analytics tools we use (such as lightweight, privacy-first services) process IP addresses in an anonymized form. Data never leaves our care, and we refresh cookie consents annually or after significant policy updates.

3. Managing your cookie preferences

You have full control. When you first visit our website, a cookie banner allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can also modify your preferences at any time via your browser settings or by clicking the “Cookie Preferences” link (available in the footer of our site). Most browsers accept cookies by default, but you can adjust settings to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact site functionality.

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data.
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions.

If you choose to reject analytics cookies, your visit remains entirely anonymous; we respect that decision as we would respect a shadow in the garden — present yet undisturbed.

4. Third-party services & embedded content

Our website may contain links to external resources, garden directories, or embedded content (such as a short video about our pruning salons). Those third-party platforms have their own cookie policies. We encourage you to review them, as we have no control over their data practices. Orvessa Root Haven does not embed tracking pixels or social media trackers that collect personal data without explicit interaction.

5. Duration & storage of cookies

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device for up to 12 months, unless deleted). Persistent cookies help us remember your preferences across repeat visits — like a perennial returning each season. You can delete all stored cookies through your browser settings at any time.

6. Updates to this Cookies Policy

Just as our garden evolves, we may occasionally refine this policy to reflect changes in technology, legal obligations, or operational needs. The “Last updated” date at the bottom of this page will indicate the latest revision. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our site after modifications implies acknowledgment of the updated terms.

If you have any questions regarding our use of cookies or digital privacy, please reach out to our data stewardship team: privacy@orvessaroothaven.com or via mail at 142 Blackwood Lane, Evergreen Valley, Ashford, CA 95684, USA.
